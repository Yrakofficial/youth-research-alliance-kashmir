About YRAK

Youth Research Alliance Kashmir (YRAK) aims to unite students, healthcare professionals, researchers and innovators to create evidence-based solutions for health challenges, especially cancer awareness and prevention. We believe that research and awareness can transform communities and save lives.

Founder's Message

Welcome to Youth Research Alliance Kashmir (YRAK). Our mission is not simply to discuss problems but to create solutions through research, awareness, innovation and community engagement. We believe that every young person has the potential to contribute towards a healthier and stronger society. Through collaborative research and public health initiatives, we aim to build a future where evidence-based knowledge drives meaningful impact across Kashmir and beyond.

Why Cancer Research Matters

Cancer remains one of the leading health challenges worldwide. Research helps improve prevention strategies, early diagnosis, treatment outcomes and patient quality of life. YRAK is committed to supporting awareness initiatives and promoting scientific research that contributes to better public health outcomes.

Research Projects

  • Cancer Awareness Status Assessment in Kashmir
  • Youth Perception Towards Cancer Screening Programs
  • Tobacco Consumption and Cancer Risk Survey
  • Community Awareness of Early Cancer Detection
  • Geospatial Mapping of Health Awareness Indicators
  • Cancer Prevention Education Initiatives
